# 2026-07-02 — Re-aim Muakkil: venture agent for non-technical founders

**Decision:** Re-aim Muakkil from a task-orchestration assistant ("speak a charge, get a research email") to a **venture agent for non-technical founders** — one agent that takes a spoken idea to a built AND distributed product. The four muakkals re-map: Scribe = idea intake, Seeker = validation, Maker = build (promoted from "coming soon" to core), Herald = distribution (domain, SEO/AEO, launch kit, email, analytics report — promoted from "sends messages" to the differentiator). The buildathon assistant scope is superseded; reusable foundations (stack, brand, Supabase/RLS, orchestrator plan→steps design) carry forward.

**Why:** The unserved gap is post-build distribution: AI builders stop at deploy; marketing tools start after brand exists; no product runs the launch/first-users half for non-technical people end-to-end. Muakkil's existing agent mythos maps one-to-one onto that journey, and the factory's codified launch playbooks are a ready-made distribution brain.

**Validation status (hard rule #1 — debt recorded, not skipped silently):** A Phase 0 validation sprint (5 Mom-Test interviews + a paid concierge pilot) was defined and approved. The operator then chose to **proceed with the Phase 1 build immediately, with validation running in parallel** — this entry records that as explicit validation debt. Gate: ≥3/5 interviewees raising the post-build stall unprompted AND ≥1 paying concierge client re-engaging after the day-7 report.

**What would prove it wrong:** The parallel validation failing its gate; or 10 beta ventures through the loop with <5 reaching ≥10 non-founder users in 30 days; or discovering the ICP won't approve agent-executed outward actions even behind approval gates.

**Revisit trigger:** Validation-gate review (~2 weeks); beta acceptance review after 10 ventures; or immediately if a major platform (Wix/Base44, Lovable) ships an agentic launch/GTM product.
