# Product Hunt Launch Playbook

## The framework in one paragraph

A Product Hunt launch is a 24-hour all-out push for upvotes + comments that, if successful, yields: a permanent top-product-of-the-day badge, the "Maker" page for SEO, a spike of 1K-5K visits, and (sometimes) genuine early customers. The vast majority of value compounds *after* launch day from the perma-badge and the listing's evergreen SEO. The launch-day work is to maximize visibility within the first 4 hours when most upvotes happen.

## When to use

- After MVP is functional + landing copy is locked + Stripe Checkout works
- When you have a warm list of 50-200 people willing to support
- When you've researched the day-of-week / season for your category

## Pre-launch (T-14 to T-1)

### Hunter (optional)
- Find a hunter with ≥5 successful launches in your category
- Reach via PH DM, X DM, or warm intro
- They post your product at 00:01 PT — boost from their followers
- Or self-launch (works, but ~15% less reach)

### Assets to prepare
- **Tagline** (≤60 chars, sharp)
- **Description** (180 chars, what + who + why now)
- **Gallery** (5-7 images: hero + 4 product screenshots + 1 social proof)
- **Maker comment** (first comment, 200-400 words, story + ask for feedback)
- **Topics** (3 max — PH limits)
- **Discussion question** (one open question to spark replies)

### The hype window (T-7)
- "Coming soon" page on PH (you can submit early)
- Tease on X with a 30-sec demo video
- Email warm list T-2: "we go live Tuesday — here's how to help"

### Day-of-launch list of 50-200 people
For each: how you know them, why they'd care, what your DM to them on launch morning says. The list is the launch. Don't skip.

## Launch day (T-0)

### 00:01 PT (PH's day starts)
- Hunter (or you) submits the product
- Product appears in the day's queue

### 06:00 PT
- Tweet thread goes live
- Reply to your earliest comments

### 08:00-09:00 PT
- Email blast to warm list (subject: "We're live on Product Hunt — would love your support")
- LinkedIn post

### 09:00 PT
- HN "Show HN" (separate timing per `hacker-news-launch.md`)

### All day
- Reply to every comment within 30 minutes
- Respond to upvoters who comment — relationships
- Don't beg for upvotes in DMs (PH detects this; throttles)
- Use DMs to share the link to specific people who'd care — no script
- Update X thread with progress, ranking, screenshots throughout the day

### 21:00 PT (last 3 hours)
- One final push — last X reply, last few personal DMs
- Don't fall asleep yet; comments still come in

## Post-launch (T+1 to T+14)

### T+1: thank-you post
- Tweet "we hit #N — here's what I learned" (transparency wins)
- LinkedIn post thanking specific supporters
- Email warm list with "what we learned + what's next"

### T+3: follow-up to signups who didn't activate
Personal DM/email: "saw you signed up from PH — what brought you in? Any blockers?"

### T+7: Sean Ellis survey
To the first cohort of activated users from PH.

### T+14: retro
- What converted (channel breakdown)
- What didn't (which DM template flopped)
- What to do differently next launch
- Save in `decisions/launch-retro-YYYY-MM-DD.md`

## Anti-patterns

- **Launching without a list.** Cold launch = ranking #20 with 50 upvotes.
- **Begging in DMs.** "Please upvote, link here" — PH detects this and throttles.
- **No maker comment.** Empty discussion = no signal of life.
- **Generic copy.** Tagline that could be any tool. Make it specific.
- **Bad timing.** Mondays + Fridays + holidays = dead. Launch Tuesday-Thursday.
- **Going dark mid-day.** Comments need 30-min responses; otherwise they disappear from feed.

## What "success" actually means

- **Top 5 of day**: badge + ~3K visits + SEO win
- **Top 10**: badge + 1K visits
- **Top 20**: smaller spike, still get the listing
- Not top 20: still get the maker page (evergreen SEO)

PMF correlation: weak. PH launches can spike from your network. Don't conflate this with PMF — see `mvp-stage/sean-ellis-survey.md`.
